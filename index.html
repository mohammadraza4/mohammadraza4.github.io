Mohammad Raza

Researcher in AI

My interests are grounded in the fields of AI and symbolic methods, with the goal of helping users perform complex tasks through natural interaction experiences such as providing examples, natural language instructions, multi-modal inputs, or fully automatic suggestions from the AI system. My experience spans a broad range of techniques, with a strong focus recently on neuro-symbolic program synthesis methods using large language models. I have led the development and productization of such techniques for task automation in a variety of domains including data science, business intelligence, office productivity, and code editing. This work has had strong practical impact in mass-market products (including Microsoft Power BI, Visual Studio Code and Excel) as well as publications in top-tier research conferences.
